SOCIAL COMPLIANCE

As an international enterprise with a high sense of social responsibility, LABEL7 GROUP always regards sustainable development as its core ideal. In the entire product life cycle from raw material procurement to manufacturing, we aim to protect the environment and safeguard the consumers& employees‘ health to the greatest extent.

Green promise

We focus on the areas that make the biggest difference to our planet: reducing the impact of our products on climate change, protecting vital resources and using more environmentally friendly materials.

Health and safety

The health and safety of our employees is our highest daily priority. Everywhere we operate, we have an unshakeable commitment to protecting the physical and mental wellbeing of our employees.

Compliance

LABEL7 GROUP complies with FSC, OEKO-TEX, ISO9001, ISO14001 environmental standards and plans to obtain GRS certification standards by 2021.

SUSTAINABLE MATERIALS

Sustainability is the key idea of LABEL7 GROUP, We are committed to developing organic, biodegradable and eco-friendly materials to ensure that everything we do has a positive and sustainable impact on the environment.
